RAG — AI on Your Enterprise Data

Connect AI to your own documents, databases, and knowledge bases — not generic internet knowledge

What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) lets an AI model answer questions using your specific data — your manuals, contracts, policies, product specs, customer records, SharePoint, Confluence, or any internal knowledge base. Instead of guessing or hallucinating, the model retrieves the exact relevant content and generates a grounded, accurate answer.

This is the difference between a generic chatbot and an AI that actually knows your business.
